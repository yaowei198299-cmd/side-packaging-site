## Product Specifications: Engineering the Perfect Apparel Box

When designing **custom gift boxes for clothes**, engineering is just as important as aesthetics. Here is a breakdown of the materials and structures we offer:

### 1. Material Selection
- **Art Paper (157gsm - 200gsm)**: Ideal for vibrant full-color printing and various laminations.
- **Kraft Paper (120gsm - 300gsm)**: Perfect for brands focusing on a "natural" or "eco-friendly" aesthetic.
- **Greyboard (800gsm - 2000gsm)**: The core material for rigid boxes, providing structural integrity and a premium weight.

### 2. Finishes and Embellishments
- **Matte Lamination**: Provides a sophisticated, non-reflective finish that feels smooth to the touch.
- **Foil Stamping (Gold/Silver/Rose Gold)**: Adds a metallic shine to logos and accents.
- **Spot UV**: Creates a contrast between matte surfaces and glossy, raised textures.
- **Soft-Touch Lamination**: Also known as "velvet finish," this is the gold standard for **luxury apparel gift boxes**.

### 3. Box Structures
- **Magnetic Flap Boxes**: These ship flat (collapsible) to save on freight but assemble into a rigid, luxury box with hidden magnets.
- **Two-Piece Drawer Boxes**: A slide-out mechanism that adds a layer of mystery and elegance.
- **Top & Bottom Lid Boxes**: The classic shoebox style, reimagined with premium materials and finishes.

## Sustainability: The FSC Advantage

For brands operating in the EU and UK, the **FSC (Forest Stewardship Council)** certification is a critical trust signal. Using **FSC-certified apparel boxes** ensures that your packaging comes from responsibly managed forests that provide environmental, social, and economic benefits.

We prioritize:
- **Plastic-Free Lamination**: Using water-based coatings instead of plastic films.
- **Soy-Based Inks**: Reducing VOC emissions during the printing process.
- **Recycled Content**: Offering boxes made from 100% post-consumer waste without compromising on strength.

## Market Analysis: USA vs. UK vs. EU

### The USA Market
In the United States, "Big and Bold" is often the strategy. American brands tend to favor larger-scale branding and vibrant colors. However, there is a growing trend toward high-end **wholesale clothing packaging** that supports the booming "Direct-to-Consumer" (DTC) fashion sector.

### The UK Market
The UK market is highly focused on "Heritage and Craftsmanship." British consumers appreciate subtle textures and traditional design elements. Keywords like "Luxury packaging UK" often see high search volume during the holiday and wedding seasons.

### The EU Market
Mainland Europe is the leader in sustainable regulations. Packaging must be not only beautiful but also compliant with strict waste management laws. German and French markets, in particular, demand high-grade **FSC-certified apparel boxes** and minimalist, multi-functional designs.
